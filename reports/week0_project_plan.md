# Week 0 Project Plan

## Problem

I often feel that I do not spend much money, but my savings do not increase as expected.

## Goal

Understand spending patterns and explore how AI can assist personal financial management.

## Approach

* Record daily expenses
* Analyze data with AI
* Generate weekly reports
* Identify opportunities for improvement

## Success Criteria

* Complete 30 days of expense tracking
* Generate 4 weekly reports
* Build an AI-assisted financial analysis workflow
